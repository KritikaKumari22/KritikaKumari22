## Current directions

- **Foundational models for biological sequences:** developing machine learning approaches for sequence classification and representation learning.
- **Protein dynamics:** using computational methods to understand how protein behavior connects structure, function, and biological context.
- **Vesicle trafficking networks:** applying graph-theoretic Boolean modeling to study the evolution and behavior of trafficking systems.

## Research philosophy

I am interested in building interpretable computational tools that make biological systems easier to compare, classify, and reason about. This template leaves space to add publications, preprints, posters, datasets, and collaborators as the site grows.
